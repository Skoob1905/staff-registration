# HandySign User Guide

## Overview

HandySign is a digital platform for managing agency staff — contracts, payslips, and client records — all in one place.

---

## For Staff Members

### Home

Your home page shows your current to-do items and a summary of your contracts and payslips.

**Registration**
If you're new, you'll see a "Register Now" button. Click it and fill in your details to complete your onboarding. Once registered, your admin can send you a contract.

**Signing a Contract**
When an admin sends you a contract, a "Sign Contract" button will appear. Click it, draw your signature, and submit. The signed document is saved automatically.

**Payslips**
When your admin uploads a payslip, it appears in the payslip section at the bottom of the page. Click "Download" to view it. New payslips are marked with a badge.

**Uploading Documents**
Use the "Upload" tab to send documents back to your agency — for example, contract responses or payslip queries. Select a category and choose your file.

---

## For Admins

### STAFF Tab

This is where you manage your team.

**Inviting Staff**
Enter an email address and click "Send Invite". The system creates an account and sends a password-setup email. The staff member will appear in "Awaiting Registration" until they complete their profile.

**Managing Registered Staff**
Once a staff member registers, they appear in the "Staff" accordion list. Expand a person to see their details.

- **Send Contract**: Click "Contract" to upload a PDF. The staff member will see a "Sign Contract" button on their home page.
- **Send Payslip**: Click "Payslip" to upload a PDF. The staff member can download it from their home page.
- **Delete Contract**: If needed, you can remove a sent contract. The staff member will need a new one.

### CLIENTS Tab

This is where you manage agency client records in bulk.

**Importing Client Data**
1. Prepare a CSV file with your client information. Each column becomes a field in the record — for example: `name`, `address`, `contact`, etc.
2. Drag and drop the file onto the upload area, or click to browse.
3. Review the preview table to make sure the data looks correct.
4. Click "Import" to save everything to the system.

Each row in your CSV becomes a separate client record. After importing, the new clients appear in the "Clients" section below.

**Viewing Clients**
The "Clients" section shows a count of how many client records you have. Click any client to expand it and see all the fields from your CSV.

**Import History**
Every CSV you upload is stored for reference. You can download any previous file from the "Import History" section at the bottom of the page.

### UPLOAD Tab

Use this to upload a document directly to a staff member's inbox. Select the staff member, choose a category (General, Contract Response, or Payslip Query), pick your file, and upload.
